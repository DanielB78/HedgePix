What HedgePix Does

HedgePix uses a machine-learning model trained on historical market behaviour. It studies how stocks move under different conditions, how groups of stocks relate to each other, and how momentum and volatility change over time.

Rather than predicting exact prices, the model produces a ranked list based on statistical patterns it has identified. Stocks near the top resemble names that historically performed well in similar environments.

How to Use HedgePix

The Rankings page displays the modelâ€™s preferred stocks for today. Higher-ranked stocks are the ones the system currently views most favourably based on its learned patterns.

Clicking a ticker adds it to the chart so you can see how its ranking changes over time. This helps you identify improving momentum, weakening signals, or unstable movement.

Experimental and Not Financial Advice

HedgePix is an experimental tool created for research and education. The model relies on past data, which may not represent future market conditions. Financial markets can behave unexpectedly and change rapidly.

Nothing on this website should be interpreted as financial advice. Real investment decisions require independent analysis, proper risk management, and professional guidance where needed.
